Light/Dark Mode

As per the Material Design Documentation, a dark theme displays dark surfaces across the majority of a UI. It's designed to be a supplemental mode to a default (or light) theme. Dark themes reduce the luminance emitted by device screens, while still meeting minimum color contrast ratios. Devices with OLED screens benefit from the ability to turn off black pixels at any time of day.
